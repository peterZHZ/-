About GhostConvert

GhostConvert is built and maintained by a single independent developer who is passionate about privacy, web technologies, and giving users full control over their data.

Why I Created GhostConvert

Like many of you, I’ve used online file converters for years – to change an iPhone photo from HEIC to JPG, to extract audio from a video clip, or to merge documents into a PDF. But every time I used one of those "free" online tools, I felt a knot in my stomach: Where is my file actually going? Who else can see it?

I discovered that most online converters upload your files to a remote server, process them, and then provide a download link. Your private photos, sensitive spreadsheets, and personal documents may sit on a third‑party server – sometimes for hours, sometimes indefinitely. That’s a privacy risk I could no longer accept.

So I built GhostConvert: a file conversion tool that works entirely inside your browser. No files are ever uploaded to any server. Every conversion – whether it’s an image, a document, or an audio file – runs locally on your device’s CPU. You can even turn off your internet connection and GhostConvert still works perfectly.

How It Works

GhostConvert leverages cutting‑edge browser technologies:

  • WebAssembly (WASM) for high‑performance image and audio processing.
  • Modern JavaScript APIs like the FileReader API, Canvas API, and AudioContext.
  • Client‑side libraries such as PDF.js, Mammoth (for DOCX), SheetJS (for XLSX), LameJS (for MP3 encoding), and HEIC2ANY (for Apple HEIC images).

All these libraries run in a secure sandbox inside your browser. That means your files never leave your device – not even for a millisecond.

Our Promise: 100% Local, 100% Private

  • ✅ No server uploads – files are processed right in your browser.
  • ✅ No hidden logs – we don't track which files you convert.
  • ✅ No registration – use the tool immediately, with zero data collection.
  • ✅ Works offline – once the page is loaded, you can disconnect the internet and everything still works.

Meet the Developer

I’m a full‑stack developer with a background in web security and client‑side performance. I built GhostConvert because I wanted a tool that respects the fundamental principle: your files are yours, and they should stay on your machine.

I believe in radical transparency. The entire codebase is front‑end only – you can inspect every line of JavaScript in your browser's DevTools. There is no backend server, no database, and no hidden tracking.

Why Advertisements?

These ads never access your files or compromise your privacy – they are simply how I can keep the tool running and improve it over time.
