Key Promise: GhostConvert is a 100% local file converter. We do not upload, store, or process your files on any remote server. All conversions happen inside your browser. Your files never leave your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Data You Provide: GhostConvert does not require registration, login, or any form submission. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal information unless you voluntarily contact us via email.

2. File Processing (What Happens to Your Files)

GhostConvert never uploads your files. The entire conversion workflow – reading, decoding, transforming, and encoding – takes place locally on your device’s CPU and memory, inside the browser’s sandbox. We have no technical ability to access, view, or store your files because they are never transmitted to any server.

4. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Functional Cookies: We use a cookie to remember your language preference (e.g., English or 简体中文) so you don't have to select it each time you visit.

  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense sets cookies to display ads (see Section 3.2).

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our and other websites.

5. Your Choices & Consent

When you first visit GhostConvert, we show a cookie consent banner asking you to accept cookies for analytics and personalized ads. You can choose:

  • Accept: We will enable Google Analytics and AdSense cookies (which include personalized ads).

If you change your mind, you can clear your browser's cookies and revisit our site to see the consent banner again.

Because GhostConvert does not upload, store, or transmit your files to any server, there is no risk of a server‑side data breach exposing your documents or images. The security of your files depends solely on the security of your own device and browser.
